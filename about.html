About

Hi, I'm Tegan Watson. I'm a recent grad and front end web developer focusing on building beautiful, responsive websites.

Further Education in Programming

I discovered my interest in programming when I took an HTML/CSS class in college, and decided to continue learning to code on my own time, in addition to my formal academic studies. I have completed the web design course at Skillcrush, and have worked up through the front end classes in The Odin Project. To fully cement my understanding of front end development before moving forward, I am currently working on completing the Front End Web Development track at Treehouse.

Writing

When I'm not writing code, I like to write other things. I majored in film and media studies and minored in creative writing, and so have pursued various styles of writing, from op ed pieces and press releases to screenwriting, novel writing, short fiction, and poetry, some of which I post on my writing blog, A Teacup Full of Wine. I worked for three years as Senior Fiction Editor for my campus literary magazine, the Wells Chronicle, and became Co-Editor-in-Chief in my junior year. My poetry has been published in The Missing Slate, (parenthetical), and Everest.
